Luna Aura is a behavioral analytics platform designed to explore how physiological and lifestyle signals influence mental wellbeing. The system analyzes daily behavioral inputs including sleep duration, perceived stress, mood state, physical activity, and menstrual cycle phase. Machine learning models trained on behavioral datasets evaluate relationships between these signals to estimate potential fluctuations in emotional wellbeing. Rather than diagnosing mental health conditions, the system provides interpretable analytics that help researchers and individuals understand how behavioral patterns interact with cognitive and emotional states.
